About Shadab

Vocalist | Performer | Songwriter

I’m Shadab ur Rehman — a vocalist rooted in the soul of Gilgit-Baltistan [1]. My music blends classical depth with modern emotion, creating soundscapes that speak from the heart.

With experience performing at live gigs, cultural events, and music gatherings, I’ve connected with diverse audiences through raw vocals and heartfelt lyrics. Each performance is a story — rooted in identity, passion, and purpose.

I sing not for applause, but for impact — to represent my roots, to heal, and to inspire.
